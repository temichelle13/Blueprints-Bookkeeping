13 - Downloadable Templates & Resources Library
What & Why
Build a curated library of professionally designed, downloadable PDF templates that website visitors can access. This positions Blueprints & Bookkeeping as a high-value authority, drives lead capture (email-gated downloads), and gives prospective clients immediate utility before they even engage. The existing single "Financial Readiness Checklist" PDF is minimal — this task replaces it with a polished version and adds many more templates covering the firm's core expertise areas.

Done looks like
A dedicated /resources page showcasing all available templates in a browsable, categorized grid
Each template card shows title, description, category, and page count
Downloads are gated behind email capture (reuses existing newsletter subscribe endpoint with signup_source: "lead_magnet")
Once email is submitted, user gets access to download that template plus all others (session-based unlock)
All PDFs are professionally generated with brand colors (deep navy #1B2A5A, periwinkle #5B5EA6), the BB logo, and clean typography
Header/footer navigation updated to include "Resources" link
Existing Financial Readiness Checklist replaced with a polished branded version
Template Ideas (10-12 templates across key categories)
Bookkeeping & Financial Organization
Monthly Close Checklist — Step-by-step procedure for closing your books each month (reconciliation, review, reporting)
Chart of Accounts Template — Industry-specific starter chart of accounts for small businesses, with explanations of each category
Expense Categorization Guide — Quick-reference guide for correctly categorizing common business expenses (with IRS category mappings)
Business Planning & Funding
SBA Loan Application Prep Checklist — Every document and data point you need before applying, organized by section
3-Year Financial Projection Worksheet — Fillable framework for revenue forecasting, expense planning, and cash flow modeling
Business Plan Outline Template — Section-by-section outline matching SBA/lender expectations, with prompts for what to write
Crypto & Digital Assets
Crypto Transaction Log Template — Structured log for tracking wallet transfers, exchange trades, staking rewards, and DeFi activity
Digital Asset Tax Prep Organizer — Checklist of everything your tax preparer needs if you hold or transact in crypto
Operations & Growth
New Business Entity Setup Checklist — Step-by-step guide for forming an LLC/Corp (Oregon-focused with nationwide notes)
Contractor vs Employee Decision Matrix — Framework for determining worker classification with key IRS factors
Cash Flow Forecast Template — 12-month rolling cash flow tracker with seasonal adjustment guidance
Client Onboarding Document Checklist — What to collect from new bookkeeping clients (for other bookkeepers or self-serve)
Out of scope
Email delivery of PDFs (downloads are direct from the site after email capture)
User accounts or saved download history
Editable/fillable form fields inside PDFs (these are reference/print templates)
CMS for managing templates (all content is static in code)
Tasks
Build the Resources page — Create /resources route with a categorized grid of template cards, email-gate modal, and session-based unlock logic so once a visitor enters their email they can download freely.
Generate all PDF templates — Create a PDF generation script using pdfkit that produces 10-12 branded templates with the BB logo, brand colors, clean layouts, and substantive content. Replace the existing minimal checklist with a polished version.
Update navigation and sitemap — Add "Resources" to header nav, footer links, and sitemap.xml. Update the Home page lead magnet section to link to the full resources library instead of just the single checklist.
